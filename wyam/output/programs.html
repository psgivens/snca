Bread Making Class

Join us for a hands on experience making bread. Take the bread dough home, let it rise, and bake it for dinner. Bread may be either white bread or Sourdough. Sourdough classes include a sample of Sourdough starter that you can take home, cultivate, and continue to make this delicious bread for your family.

$5 for Stoneview Nature Center Associates members, $10 for non-members. All ages welcome.

Instructors: Phillip Scott Givens and Irene Vidyanti (https://www.instagram.com/delayedecho/).

Modern Mindfulness Practice Group

Join us as we explore a creative, flexible and evidence-based approach to mindfulness meditation based on the teachings of Shinzen Young's Unified Mindfulness. All levels of experience are welcome. All sessions will include formal seated practice, walking meditation, and discussion. FREE

As weather permits we practice outdoors, enjoying the beauty of the urban sanctuary landscape. Meditation cushions and chairs provided.

Instructor: Marilyn McLaughlin, MFA,Somatic Movement Therapist, Mindfulness Coach L2 (https://www.playavistamindfulness.com/)

Body Balance

Improve your mind, your body, and your life through New BodyBalance -- a blend of Tai Chi, Yoga, and Pilates. This class promotes toning, flexibility, spinal alignment, balance, posture, and core strength. Low impact on the joints -- High impact to get heart rate pumping.
